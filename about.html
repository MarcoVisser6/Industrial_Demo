ABOUT US

Industrial Demo Sales was founded with the intention of being the worlds eminent supplier of industrial equipment, tools, and services. Our founder and managing director, Luffy C. Nickson, had a vision of a company that provides local businesses across all manufacturing sectors with suitable products and equipments for effective business operations. This is supplied by the extensive catalog of brands we represent, suited to our client’s specific need or request. Accompanying this is the support of expert technical assistance in keeping manufacturer production efficient and cost effective.

OUR VISION

To be a knowledgeable supplier of vital industrial equipment, tools, and services, substantiated by superior service and the highest quality products that satisfy our client’s needs.

OUR MISSION

In order to assure that our supplier to customer partnership is of mutual care, satisfaction and confidence. Both our external and internal customers must sense the assurance that their best interests are our paramount concern and that Industrial Demo Sales commits 100% to customers’ satisfaction.
